How to Play

×

Drag new arrows from the large container in the upper right corner of the game board, and drop them into the grid. The smaller container below that shows you your next new arrow. You can swap these two arrows with the 🔁 button located under your score, as long as the number indicated within that button is positive.

Combine, cancel, and merge compatible pairs of adjacent arrows in the grid to earn points. The goal is to get as many points as possible until you can no longer place new arrows. Here are a few examples of these actions. Hover over or tap the examples to play an animation.
